A-values for teams that played the 2015 ACF Regionals are listed in the table below. Team will be invited to play 2015 ACF Nationals at the University of Michigan based on their A-values. Details of A-value calculations are announced here.

Please contact 2015 ACF Nationals Tournament Director Matt Weiner or ACF Communications Officer Stephen Eltinge with questions.

These teams have been granted Automatic Bids:
Harvard University, Rice University, Stanford University, University of Alberta, University of Oxford.

Teams in blue background qualified by winning their site. Teams in yellow highlights qualified as the top-placing Undergraduate team at a site with four or more Undergraduate teams. Teams are sorted in descending order of A-value. To view the raw-data that went into A-value calculations, please click on the "calculations" tab.
