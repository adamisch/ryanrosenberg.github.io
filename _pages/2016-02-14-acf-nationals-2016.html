The main announcement for ACF Nationals 2016, to be held on the weekend of April 16-17 at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor can be found here.

A-values for teams that played the 2016 ACF Regionals are listed in the table below. Team will be invited to play 2016 ACF Nationals at the University of Michigan based on their A-values. Details of A-value calculations are announced here.

Please contact 2016 ACF Nationals Tournament Director Jerry Vinokurov or ACF Communications Officer Stephen Eltinge with questions.

Teams in blue background qualified by winning their site. Teams in yellow highlights qualified as the top-placing Undergraduate team at a site with four or more Undergraduate teams. Teams are sorted in descending order of A-value.
